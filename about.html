About TextToolBox

TextToolBox is an All-in-One Global Utility Hub designed for writers, developers, SEO specialists, and students who need fast, secure, and reliable text manipulation.

Our Vision

We saw a world where text tools were cluttered with ads, slow to load, and questionable on privacy. We built TextToolBox to be the opposite: Lightning Fast, 100% Private, and Beautifully Designed.

Who We Are

We are a team of software engineers based in New Zealand, dedicated to building the "Boring Utilities" that power the web. TextToolBox is our flagship project for global text processing.

Free Forever

Our tools are free, and they always will be. We support the site through minimal, non-intrusive advertising to keep our servers running and our tools updated.
