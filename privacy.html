Privacy Policy

At TextToolBox, your privacy is our core priority. Our entire business model is built on the fact that your text is your own.

1. No Text Logging

TextToolBox does NOT store or log any text you paste into our tools. All processing (case conversion, word counting, slug generation) happens in your browser's local memory and is immediately cleared when you refresh the page.

2. AdSense Transparency

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

3. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@texttoolbox.net.
